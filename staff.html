Our Staff

Paula Slater

Dean

Paula Slater, the Dean of STEAM Academy is from Stanford University in California, where she was Professor and Chair of Biology as well as Director of Curriculum and Assessment. While keeping biology chair, she also led the Health Sciences Department, and she held the endowed Hollis Chair of Health and Wellness, which included responsibility for overseeing the efforts of students in STEAM majors.

Science Teacher

I have an M.S. in Ecology, Evolution, Natural Resources, a minor in Spanish, and a certificate in Environmental Geomatics. I have taught lessons in ecology and evolution to 1-5 students as an outdoor educator and at the middle school level.
